# AI Integration Roadmap

This document outlines how Large Language Models (LLMs), specifically Claude, will be integrated into the agricultural machinery valuation ecosystem.

## 1. Natural Language Insights
Using AI to transform raw valuation data into human-readable reports for farmers and traders.
- *Example:* "Based on the 15% depreciation trend in the 2024 tractor market, your asset's value is holding 5% above average due to low usage hours."

## 2. Visual Condition Assessment
Integrating computer vision to analyze machinery photos. AI can detect:
- Exterior wear and tear.
- Engine component health via image analysis.
- Cabin maintenance status.

## 3. Market Sentiment Analysis
Scraping and analyzing global agricultural news and trade bulletins to adjust the valuation algorithm for local market shifts.

## 4. Technical Support Bot
A Claude-powered assistant to help users understand the technical reasons behind a specific valuation.
